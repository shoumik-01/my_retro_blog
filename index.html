About Me

Hello there! I'm Shoumik Hasan, a dangerously over-caffeinated grad student based in Minnesota, bravely navigating the chaotic intersection of creativity and existential dread. I'm passionate about arts, engineering, natural sciences, mild chaos, and the eternal quest to find the perfect playlist for every mood imaginable!

When I'm not wrangling words or overthinking my next sentence, you'll find me chasing down ideas like they owe me money, or trying to make sense of this strange little planet we all share. This blog is my corner of the internet—less a digital diary, more a collection of mildly organized thoughts and hot takes that no one asked for but you're getting anyway.

Welcome to the show. No refunds.
